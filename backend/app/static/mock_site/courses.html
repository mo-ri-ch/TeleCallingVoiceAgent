Courses & Fees

Certified Financial Planner (CFP) Program

Our flagship 6-month CFP program covers retirement planning, tax planning, estate planning, and investment management. Classes run on weekday evenings and weekend mornings to suit working professionals.

The total course fee for the CFP program is ₹52,000, payable in two installments. The fee includes study material, mock exams, and one year of exam-retake support.

Equity Research Analyst Program

A 4-month intensive program focused on financial modelling, valuation, and sector research, with a guaranteed placement interview at the end of the course.
